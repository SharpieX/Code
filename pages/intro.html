About the team

We,at Integrated Learning Centre, attempt to provide an alternative to traditional education system with the help of technology. A place where we thrive to achieve universal education and develop critical thinking in our children. We are a team of education enthusiasts who aim at restructuring the mode of education delivery. An online platform that is affordable as well as accessible to all A dedicated faculty trying to innovate with teaching methods will now be available to our children just a click away. Currently we are focusing in the field of Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with a future vision of delving into all the fields relevant to a child's bright future.
